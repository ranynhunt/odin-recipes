Sloppy Steaks

Description:

Just a big ol' cut of juicy red meat with water dumped all over it.

They can't stop you from ordering a steak and a glass of water!

Ingredients:

  • Juicy red meat, 1 lb
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 cup water, iced

Steps:

  1. Dry meat with paper towel
  2. Season both sides with salt and pepper
  3. Cook on 400 degree griddle 3-4 minutes per side, until medium rare
  4. Let rest for 5 min
  5. Serve on plate with nothing else, no garnish
  6. Add iced water to glass
  7. SLOP 'EM UP!!
